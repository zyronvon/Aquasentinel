AquaSentinel is a research-driven project dedicated to detecting microplastics in water samples using advanced AI technology. Built with state-of-the-art CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) models and open-source tools, our platform contributes towards cleaner oceans and a sustainable future for marine ecosystems.

We believe that by combining cutting-edge artificial intelligence with environmental science, we can provide researchers, scientists, and conservationists with the tools they need to understand and combat plastic pollution in our oceans.
